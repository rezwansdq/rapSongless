How to Play Songless Unlimited

The Goal

Guess the song title and artist based on short audio snippets. The snippets get longer with each incorrect guess or skip!

Gameplay

  • Listen: Each round starts with a very short snippet of a song.
  • Guess: Type your guess into the search bar. Autocomplete will help you find the right song.
  • Submit: Press 'SUBMIT' to check your guess.
  • Skip: Can't guess? Hit 'SKIP' to move to the next stage. The snippet gets longer!
  • Stages: There are 5 stages in total. If you can't guess after the final stage, you fail the round.

Winning & Losing

  • Win: Guess correctly at any stage to win the round! You'll hear the full preview.
  • Lose: If you use all stages without guessing, you'll fail. The correct song will be revealed.

Input Modes

Choose to play with songs from a specific Artist, a Top Chart Genre, or try the Daily Song Challenge.

Volume Control

Adjust the game volume at any time via the settings gear icon in the top right corner of the game screen.

Good luck and have fun!
